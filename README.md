Stability Capitalism: The AI Dividend that Prevents the Automation Cliff
A policy framework for funding economic stability through commercial AI infrastructure
Overview
This 60-page white paper proposes a market-based mechanism to prevent economic destabilization from rapid AI-driven workforce displacement. The framework introduces a micro-fee on commercial AI inference to fund direct dividend payments to working Americans.
Key Elements
The Mechanism: A 0.054-cent fee per commercial AI inference, collected at the cloud infrastructure level.
The Dividend: $3,000 monthly payment to working Americans earning under $75,000 annually.
The Model: Structured on the proven Alaska Permanent Fund framework, adapted for AI-era economics.
Implementation: Includes a complete Ohio pilot program design and 18-month implementation timeline.
